Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

Tridom Contractors was founded in 2010 by a team of passionate engineers and builders with a single vision: to raise the standard of construction in Zimbabwe. What began as a small civil works contractor has grown into a multi-disciplinary construction company delivering projects across the country.

Today, we employ over 50 skilled professionals and maintain a fleet of modern construction equipment. Our projects range from multi-storey commercial developments in Harare's CBD to rural infrastructure programmes that transform communities.
