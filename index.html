About Tridom

Zimbabwe's Most Trusted Construction Partner

Founded in 2010, Tridom Contractors has grown from a local building firm into one of Zimbabwe's most respected construction and civil engineering companies. We deliver projects on time, within budget, and to the highest standards of quality — every single time.

Our team of licensed engineers, site managers, and skilled tradespeople brings together decades of combined experience across commercial, residential, and infrastructure projects.
